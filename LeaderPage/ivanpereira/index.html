Ivan Pereira

Director of Cruise Talent Acquisition and Business Develompment

Perwita Nusaraya

"With over 25 years in the cruise industry, Ivan Pereira serves as the Director of Cruise Talent Acquisition and Business Development at Perwita Nusaraya Group, ensuring the seamless hiring and deployment of maritime professionals. His expertise spans operations, strategic planning, and recruitment for both contemporary and ultra-luxury cruise lines (Ocean & River).

Ivan's leadership has been instrumental in enhancing service standards and streamlining processes. He has managed high-volume hiring, from sourcing to deploying top-quality candidates. His experience includes 15 years onboard Royal Caribbean Group ships and shoreside roles as a Lead Trainer and Global Recruiter for the same company.

He has also been the Cruise Department Manager for Royal Caribbean Group’s official hiring partner and Compliance & Business Development Manager for MSC’s official hiring partner. Ivan's comprehensive understanding of talent acquisition processes and strategic planning capabilities underscores Perwita Nusaraya Group's leadership in maritime recruitment."
