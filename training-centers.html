    Perwita Nusaraya Training Center is a premier institution dedicated to the development of skilled professionals across various industries.

    With a strong focus on both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, the center offers a diverse range of training programs, including Maritime Training, Caregiver Training, Fishing Vessel Training, and Construction Skill Training.

    Maritime Training Center

    The Maritime Training Center of Perwita Nusaraya Group is a premier institution dedicated to the education and training of maritime professionals in Indonesia. Established to enhance the skills and competencies of seafarers, the center offers a comprehensive range of courses, including Basic Safety Training, Medical First Aid, and Crisis Management.


    • BASIC SAFETY TRAINING (BST)
    • SURVIVAL CRAFT AND RESCUE BOAT (SCRB)
    • MEDICAL FIRST AID (MFA)
    • CRISIS MANAGEMENT AND HUMAN BEHAVIOUR TRAINING (CMHBT)
    • SECURITY AWARENESS TRAINING (SAT)
    • PROGRAM REVALIDASI ( BST, PSCRB )

    Foreign Language and culture

    The Foreign Language and Culture Training Center of Perwita Nusaraya Group is an esteemed institution focused on equipping individuals with essential language skills and cultural understanding for international opportunities. Specializing in Japanese language and cultural education, the center offers meticulously designed programs that cover language proficiency, cultural nuances, and professional etiquette.


    • Japanese Language Program
    • Korean Language Program
    • English Language Program

    Caregiver Training

    The Caregiver Training Program at Perwita Nusaraya Group is dedicated to preparing individuals for rewarding careers in the healthcare and caregiving sectors. This program provides comprehensive training in essential caregiving skills, including patient care, first aid, hygiene, and elderly care.

    • Personal Basic care techniques
    • Proper bathing techniques
    • Lift and carry techniques
    • Clinical skills like tube feeding and catheter care

    Fishing Vessel Training

    The Fishing Vessel Training Program at Perwita Nusaraya Group is designed to equip individuals with the skills and knowledge required for successful careers in the fishing industry. This comprehensive program covers a wide range of topics, including navigation, fishing techniques, safety protocols, and vessel maintenance.

    • Catching and Fishing Equipment & Operation
    • Fish Processing
    • Maintenance and Repairing Fishing Equipment

    CONSTRUCTION SKILL TRAINING

    The Construction Skill Training Program at Perwita Nusaraya Group is a specialized initiative aimed at developing proficient professionals for the construction industry. This program offers a robust curriculum that includes essential construction skills such as masonry, carpentry, plumbing, electrical work, and safety practices.

    • Carpentry
    • Barbending
    • Bricklaying
    • Plastering
