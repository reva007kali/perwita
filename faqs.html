Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Working Abroad

What are the common job opportunities for Indonesians working abroad?

Common job opportunities include positions in hotels, as caregivers, maids, factory workers, and security staff.

What is the average salary for Indonesians working abroad?

Salaries vary depending on the country and job type. For example, caregivers in Hong Kong earn around HKD 4,630 per month, while factory workers in Taiwan earn approximately TWD 23,800 per month.

Necessary Documents for Indonesians

What documents are required for Indonesians to work abroad?

Key documents include a valid passport, work visa, employment contract, health certificate, and sometimes a police clearance certificate.

How can Indonesians obtain a work visa for a foreign country?

Indonesians can obtain a work visa by securing a job offer from a foreign employer who will sponsor the visa application process. They must also meet the specific visa requirements of the destination country.

Travel Regulations

What are the travel regulations for Indonesians going abroad?

Travel regulations include having a valid passport, a visa for the destination country, and meeting any specific health and safety requirements such as vaccinations or COVID-19 tests.

Are there any restrictions on the amount of money that can be carried abroad?

Yes, Indonesians must declare if they are carrying more than IDR 100 million or its equivalent in foreign currency when traveling abroad.

Cruise Line Crew

What are the requirements to work as a cruise line crew member?

Requirements include a valid passport, seafarer’s medical certificate, STCW certificates, relevant experience, and proficiency in English.

What is the average salary for a cruise line crew member?

Salaries vary by position and cruise line, but entry-level positions often start at around USD 1,000 per month, with experienced staff earning significantly more.

River Cruise

What is life like on board a river cruise as a crew member?

Life on a river cruise includes providing exceptional service to guests, working in a close-knit team, and having the opportunity to explore various destinations along the river.

What are some popular river cruise destinations?

Popular destinations include the Rhine, Danube, Seine, and Mississippi rivers, offering scenic views and cultural experiences.

Expedition Cruise

What is an expedition cruise?

An expedition cruise is a type of cruise focused on exploration and adventure in remote areas, such as the Arctic, Antarctic, and the Galápagos Islands.

What are the challenges of working on an expedition cruise?

Challenges include working in extreme weather conditions, dealing with limited communication with the outside world, and requiring specialized skills for operating in remote areas.

Other Information

How can I apply for jobs abroad?

You can apply through international job portals, recruitment agencies, or directly through the employer’s website. It’s important to ensure the job offer is legitimate and meets all legal requirements.

What support is available for Indonesians working abroad?

Support includes assistance from Indonesian embassies and consulates, legal aid services, and organizations that help with employment rights and welfare.
