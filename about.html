    • About us

    Perwita Nusaraya Group specializes in Human Resources Development, focusing on overseas recruitment for maritime and land-based roles.

    We own a Maritime Training Center and a Construction Skill Training Center, and manage a Japanese Internship Program. With offices in Indonesia and South Korea, they ensure effective recruitment and training.

    Our History

    The Perwita Nusaraya group of companies was founded in August 1994 with the mission to uplift Indonesians through educational training and job placement. Our headquarters is located in the suburbs of Surabaya, the second-largest city in Indonesia. Our recruitment and training offices are strategically located across the country to effectively serve our crew and clients.

    Milestone

    1994

    Established

    Established in August 1994 to carry out activities of Human Resources Development in Indonesia.

    2000

    Manpower Recruiting

    Approved by The Ministry of Manpower as an Indonesian Manpower Recruiting company for Overseas placement.

    2015

    Collaborated with global companies for seafarers' recruitment.

    Endorsed crew for notable cruise lines including Carnival Cruise Lines, Oceania Cruises, Regent Seven Seas Cruises, Quark Expeditions, and Scylla.

    2018

    Established PT. Perwita Maritim Pershada, a Maritime Training Center.

    Center adheres to STCW 2010 standards. Approved by the Directorate General of Sea Transportation, Indonesia.

    2019

    Established PT. Hamerta Sinar Surya, a Construction Skill Training and Test Center.

    Recognized by CIDB Malaysia for upgrading the competency of skilled workers.

    2020

    Received approval from the Ministry of Manpower of Indonesia to conduct an Internship Program to Japan.

    Program managed by LPK Perwita Nusaraya as a Legal Sending Organization.
