    • About us

    Perwita Nusaraya Group are specializes in Human Resources Development, focusing on overseas recruitment for maritime and land-based roles.

    We own a Maritime Training Center and a Construction Skill Training Center, and manage a Japanese Internship Program. With offices in Indonesia and South Korea, they ensure effective recruitment and training.

    Milestone

    Since its inception in 1994, Perwita Nusaraya Group has achieved significant milestones in Human Resources Development.

    1994

    Established

    Established in August 1994 to carry out activities of Human Resources Development in Indonesia.

    2000

    Manpower Recruiting

    Approved by The Ministry of Manpower as an Indonesian Manpower Recruiting company for Overseas placement.

    2015

    Seafarers Agency

    We collaborated with global companies for seafarers recruitment, endorsing crew for Carnival Cruise Lines, Oceania Cruises, Regent Seven Seas Cruises, Quark Expeditions, Scylla

    2018

    PT. Perwita Maritim Pershada

    Established PT. Perwita Maritim Pershada, a Maritime Training Center based on STCW 2010 and approved by Directorate General of Sea Transportation, Indonesia

    2019

    PT. Hamerta Sinar Surya

    PT. HAMERTA SINAR SURYA a Construction Skill Training and Test Center, and recognized by CIDB Malaysia to upgrade skill worker's competency

    2020

    Internship Program

    Having approval by the Ministry of Manpower of Indonesia to conduct Internship Program to Japan, managed by LPK Perwita Nusaraya as Legal Sending Organization
