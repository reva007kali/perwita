Welcome to Perwita Nusaraya Group

Since our founding in 1994, our mission has been to empower individuals and communities through exceptional training, employment opportunities, and career development. Over the years, we have expanded our services to include maritime training, overseas internships, and comprehensive recruitment solutions.

Our success is built on the trust and support of our clients and partners, and we remain committed to integrity, respect, and continuous improvement. Thank you for being a part of our journey. We look forward to achieving great things together.
