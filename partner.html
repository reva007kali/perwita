    Why Choose Perwita Nusaraya

    Choosing Perwita Nusaraya means partnering with a company that brings decades of expertise and a proven track record in human resource development, particularly in the maritime and land-based industries. With over 30 years of experience, Perwita Nusaraya has established itself as a leader in recruitment, training, and talent management, consistently delivering top-quality personnel to a wide range of global clients.

    1. Industry Expertise and Experience


    • Decades of Success: Since 1994, Perwita Nusaraya has been at the forefront of recruitment and training in Indonesia, providing skilled professionals for both sea-based and land-based industries.

    • Specialized Training: Our state-of-the-art Maritime Training Center and Construction Skill Training and Test Center ensure that our candidates are equipped with the latest skills and knowledge to excel in their roles.

    2. Comprehensive Services


    • End-to-End Recruitment: We handle every aspect of the recruitment process, from sourcing and screening to training and deployment, ensuring that our clients receive the best possible candidates.

    • Global Reach: With strategically located offices in Indonesia and South Korea, and strong partnerships across Japan, Korea, and Malaysia, we can effectively meet the demands of international markets.

    3. Quality and Compliance


    • High Standards: We adhere to the highest industry standards, ensuring that our candidates meet the specific requirements of each client.

    • Regulatory Compliance: Perwita Nusaraya holds all necessary licenses and certifications, guaranteeing full compliance with local and international regulations..
