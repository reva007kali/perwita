    Recruitment

    Entry-level Positions Recruiters will concentrate on sourcing candidates for entry-level roles. They will be supported by the sourcing team, which will assist in recommending candidates from the Perwita Nusaraya job portal, social media platforms, and direct applications

    The Mid to High-level Positions Recruiter will exclusively target roles from Chef de Partie (CDP) upwards. They will collaborate with the sourcing coordinator, utilizing resources like LinkedIn, JobStreet,the Perwita Nusaraya job portal etc. to identify and attract potential candidates

    The Sourcing Team will leverage platforms such as LinkedIn Recruiter Lite, JobStreet, and the Perwita Nusaraya job portal, among others, to identify potential candidates and recommend them to the Recruiters.

    The Recruitment Integration Team is responsible for the administrative components of the recruitment process. They provide comprehensive guidance to candidates, from the initial stages through to post-interview follow-ups, culminating in the issuance of the employee ID.

    Marine Recruiter: Collaborating with the sourcing team, the recruiter focuses on Marine roles. He engages with Marine training schools and crew groups to find suitable candidates.

    • Entry level position recruiter
    • Mid to high level position recruiter

    Document Readiness

    Document Readiness Team steps in post-creation of the employee ID, ensuring that new hires are document-ready and prepared to join on the scheduled date.

    They serve as the primary contact for new hires during this phase, coordinating with the Visa and document assistance teams to facilitate timely readiness for boarding.

    In case of cancellations, NHRT is equipped to recommend replacements who are fully prepared. A Data Analyst will provide support to the NHRT

    • Visa Team
    • Document Assistance Team
    • Data Analyst

    Deployment

    The deployment team will communicate with the scheduling team of the client. They will be in contact with the new hire until they have reached onboard as scheduled.

    Long Term Team (Stage 1): This team conducts evaluations 1-2 months before the scheduled start date to determine if new hires are ready or facing challenges that could impede their planned joining.

    Short Term Team (Stage 2): This team assesses the new joiners’ readiness 1 to 2 weeks prior to their commencement date.
